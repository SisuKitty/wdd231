Overview

Sheena Gibbons Portfolio

For a clear title to the viewer to remember the name and know the purpose of the creation of the site and what it contains.

Purpose

To provide a place that efficiently portrays the designer's projects, experience and to offer services to potential clients and communicate their skills to potential employers.

Audience

Young adults to middle aged adults who are starting up a business or the hiring team of a company looking to potentially hire onto their team.

Scenarios

How can I see a demonstration of your understanding of design principles?

Why should we hire you?

What makes you unique from all of the other people out there I could hire?

I have always had a deep love for art, ever since I was a child I have loved to use my artistic skills to make others happy, as I child I would make fun typographic name tags for my classmates, and now I have expanded my skills to create even greater things that help others bring their dreams to life.
